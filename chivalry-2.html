Chivalry 2

MP Melee Slasher • 2021

The sequel to the 2012 classic, Chivalry 2 is a multiplayer first‑person melee fighting game that aims to bring accessibility to the “hard to learn, hard to master” slasher genre.

Coming onto the project mid‑development, I filled in missing features such as:

  • Combat design support and implementation
  • Gamepad design and implementation (layout, auto‑assist, QoL features)
  • Progression design and implementation (global, class, and weapon XP curves)
  • Balance support and Redash query construction and maintenance
  • Customization item pricing and store maintenance

Notable Mentions:

  • Cross‑platform across PSN, PC, and Xbox
  • Received an 82 OpenCritic rating
  • Over 1 M sold in the first 2 months
