Warframe

Looter Shooter • 2018

Warframe is a free‑to‑play third‑person action RPG set in a continually evolving sci‑fi world. As a technical designer on the project I contributed to the Fortuna open‑world expansion, developing and refining the bounty and quest systems and helping implement new reward structures.

I also assisted with the Railjack expansion, designing and balancing ship combat, progression, and resource systems. These updates, some of the largest and best‑received in Warframe’s history, introduced varied gameplay and extended the game’s longevity.
